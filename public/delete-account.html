Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your Gatherli account and all associated data directly from the app, or by contacting us at admin@gatherli.org.

How to delete your account from the app

1
Open Gatherli and sign in

Make sure you are logged into the account you want to delete.

2
Go to your Profile

Tap the profile icon in the bottom navigation bar.

3
Open Settings

Tap the settings icon in the top-right corner of your profile page.

4
Tap "Delete Account"

Scroll to the bottom of the Settings page and tap Delete Account.

5
Confirm deletion

Read the confirmation dialog and tap Confirm to permanently delete your account.

⚠️ This action is permanent. Once confirmed, your account and all personal data will be deleted and cannot be recovered.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed within 30 days:

Data type Action
Account credentials (email, password) Deleted immediately
Profile information (name, photo) Deleted within 30 days
Friends list and friend requests Deleted within 30 days
Group memberships Deleted within 30 days
ELO rating and rating history Deleted within 30 days
Game participation records Anonymised — game results retained
Training session participation Anonymised — session records retained

Request deletion by email

If you are unable to access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing us at admin@gatherli.org from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 30 days.

Contact us
Email: admin@gatherli.org
We respond within 5 business days.
