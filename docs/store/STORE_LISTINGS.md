### Full Description *(4000 chars max)*
```
Gatherli is the all-in-one app for sports groups — organize games, track your performance, and grow your competitive network with friends.

🏐 ORGANIZE GAMES & TRAINING
Create games or training sessions in seconds. Invite your group, manage RSVPs, handle waitlists, and get notified the moment something changes. Set up competitive games with ELO rankings, or casual practice sessions without rating impact.

👥 PRIVATE GROUPS
Create private groups for your crew. Invite friends by email or share a link — they'll be onboarded directly into your group. Manage members, assign admins, and keep your community organized.

📊 COMPETITIVE ELO RANKINGS
Every game counts. Gatherli tracks your ELO rating after every result, so you always know where you stand. See your rating history over 30 days, 90 days, or all time.

🏆 DEEP PERFORMANCE ANALYTICS
Go beyond wins and losses. Gatherli shows you:
• Win rate, streaks, and games played
• Best teammate and nemesis stats
• Head-to-head breakdowns against specific players
• How you perform in different team roles — leading, balanced, or playing with stronger partners
• Peak ELO and best wins

🎯 TRAINING SESSIONS
Schedule structured training sessions with exercise plans and participant tracking. After each session, teammates can leave anonymous feedback on intensity, coaching quality, and exercise effectiveness — helping everyone improve.

🤝 BUILD YOUR COMMUNITY
Add friends, send and receive friend requests, and build your sports network. Only friends can be invited to your groups, keeping your community trusted and tight-knit.

🔔 SMART NOTIFICATIONS
Get notified about new games, invitations, results, and training sessions. Set quiet hours to pause notifications when you don't want to be disturbed.

🌍 AVAILABLE IN 5 LANGUAGES
English, French, German, Spanish, and Italian.

Download Gatherli and start playing.
```
